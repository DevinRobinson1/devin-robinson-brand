The Ecosystem
CAIO + Fund Flow OS

Fund Flow is a separate subscription ($97/mo) -- not bundled into CAIO pricing. The CAIO configures and optimizes your Fund Flow setup as part of the retainer.

Why They're Separate

Bundling devalues both. The CAIO retainer is worth $2,500-$10,000/mo for strategic leadership and builds. Fund Flow is worth $97/mo for the platform. The CAIO engagement is the reason they discover Fund Flow. The software is the reason they stay.
