  • You're running a real estate operation -- fund, flips, rentals, wholesale, or agency -- and know AI is the next leverage point.
  • You want tactics, not theory. You'll open something, implement it Monday, and see results by Friday.

FAQ

Quick answers.

How much does it cost?
Nothing. The newsletter is free and will stay free. If something paid ever shows up in your inbox from me, it'll be because you asked for it -- not because you subscribed here.
How often do you send?
One email every Tuesday. That's it. No "just checking in" drips, no 4-email launch sequences. One issue, same format, every week.
Do I need to be technical to use what's in here?
No. Every prompt is copy-paste. Every tool is evaluated from an operator's seat, not an engineer's. If an automation needs setup, I walk through it step by step.
Is this just rebranded AI content?
No. Everything I share is either something I'm personally running in my businesses or something I've tested against the alternative. If it didn't earn its place in my stack, it doesn't earn a mention in the newsletter.
Can I unsubscribe easily?
One click at the bottom of any email. No guilt, no "are you sure," no reactivation pop-ups.
