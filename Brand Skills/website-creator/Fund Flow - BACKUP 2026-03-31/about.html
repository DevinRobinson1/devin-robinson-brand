About

The founder

Devin Robinson

Grew up in Englewood, LA. Single mom working three jobs. Nobody in my world talked about Wall Street or managing investment funds.

Built multiple businesses. Went through bankruptcy. Lost partners. Kept building. Raised $10M+ in real estate capital personally. Did 300+ deals. Lost $150K from missed follow-ups -- and built Fund Flow to make sure no operator ever has that problem again.

Fund Flow OS is the platform I wish existed when I started. Not another CRM. An autonomous operating system that does the work a $150K hire would do -- for $97/month.

Our values

How we build.

Operator-first

Every feature starts with a real operator's problem. If it doesn't save you time or make you money, it doesn't ship.

Pain-led, not feature-led

We don't sell "AI-powered platforms." We solve bleeding-neck problems: finding capital, closing missed follow-ups, and launching funds fast.

Radical transparency

We build in public. We share wins and losses. Our community watches the grind, failures, and comebacks in real time.

Compounding moats

Every feature makes the next one more valuable. Compliance history, lender intelligence, and network effects create unchurnable lock-in.
