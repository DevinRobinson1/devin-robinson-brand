FAQ

Common questions about syndications.

Is this a template or custom legal work?

Custom. Our AI generates documents tailored to your specific deal structure, fees, and terms. Then a licensed SEC attorney reviews every page. This is not a DIY template.

What type of offerings does this cover?

We support 506(b) and 506(c) Regulation D offerings — the two most common exemptions for real estate syndications and funds.

Can I use this for a fund, not just a single deal?

Yes. Whether you are syndicating a single asset or launching a blind pool fund, the 7-Day Syndication service covers your formation documents.

What if I need revisions after the documents are delivered?

One round of revisions is included. Additional revisions or material structural changes are handled at a reduced hourly rate with our attorney.

Do I need my own attorney too?

Our service covers the formation documents. We always recommend operators consult their own counsel for state-specific blue sky filings and ongoing compliance questions.
