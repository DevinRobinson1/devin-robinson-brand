What Is SyndicationPro?

SyndicationPro (formerly SponsorCloud) is a real estate syndication platform founded in 2018, based in Salt Lake City, UT. It provides GP and LP investor portals, a SEC-compliant CRM, built-in e-sign, ACH and check distribution processing, K-1 document management, co-sponsor modules, and access to the SponsorNetwork investor community. Pricing is custom and based on Equity Under Management (EUM), with no publicly listed rates. A demo is required to get a quote.

SyndicationPro was built for the established syndicator. The operator who has closed deals, has an LP list, and needs a professional platform to manage the relationships and paperwork that come with active capital operations.

The platform covers the full syndication workflow:

  • GP and LP investor portals: Dedicated portal access for both general partners and limited partners, with document sharing and investor updates
  • SEC-compliant CRM tools: Investor contact management and communication tracking
  • Built-in e-sign: Subscription document workflows directly in the platform, mature and reliable
  • ACH and check distributions: Distribution processing via both ACH and check payment methods
  • K-1 upload and management: Year-end tax document distribution to LPs
  • Co-sponsor module: Tools for joint venture raises and co-GP structures, which is a standout feature
  • SponsorNetwork: Access to a community of investors actively looking for syndication opportunities
  • Dedicated account manager: Included at their paid tiers, which is not common at this price point
  • Email marketing with high deliverability: Built-in broadcast email with deliverability infrastructure
  • Soft commitment and reservation system: Allow investors to reserve positions before official close

SyndicationPro is purpose-built for established syndicators managing active LP relationships, distributions, and document workflows.

SyndicationPro's longevity matters. Being in the market since 2018 means their product has been through multiple market cycles and refined based on real operator feedback. Their e-sign workflow is mature, their K-1 management is reliable, and their co-sponsor module fills a gap that most platforms ignore entirely.

The biggest catch is pricing. SyndicationPro does not publish rates publicly. Pricing is based on Equity Under Management (EUM) and requires a direct sales conversation. That opacity can be frustrating when you're trying to evaluate platforms without a sales call. Based on user reviews, the platform is described as competitively priced for what it offers, but you will not know your number until you contact their team.
