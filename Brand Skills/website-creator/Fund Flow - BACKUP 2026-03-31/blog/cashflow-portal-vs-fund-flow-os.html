What Is Cash Flow Portal?

Cash Flow Portal is a Y Combinator-backed real estate investor management platform designed for general partners and syndicators. It provides an investor portal, CRM, distribution automation, K-1 document management, and deal underwriting tools. The platform has helped hundreds of GPs raise over $40 billion and offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Cash Flow Portal was built by syndicators who got tired of the same investor management problems every GP faces: scattered spreadsheets, manual distribution calculations, and investor portals that look like they were designed in 2009.

The platform covers the core workflow of a real estate syndication:

  • Deal creation and management: Set up deal pages where investors can review property stats, documents, and updates
  • Investor CRM: Track contacts, investment history, and communications
  • Distributions: Automate distribution calculations and payments
  • K-1 automation: Generate and distribute tax documents without the manual grind
  • Investor portal: White-label portal where LPs log in, see positions, and access documents
  • Deal underwriting tools: Built-in deal analysis and AI-powered underwriting

Cash Flow Portal is purpose-built for syndicators managing active LP relationships and distributions.

Cash Flow Portal's Y Combinator backing gives it real credibility. YC doesn't invest in bad products. And the $40 billion in capital raised across their customer base is genuine social proof, not marketing copy.

According to G2 reviews, their customer support is consistently rated best-in-class, with 12 hours of live support Monday through Friday. That matters when you're running live capital operations.

The pricing model also offers a per-deal alternative: pay nothing upfront and $1,000 per year per property after you close. For operators still proving out their model, that structure has real appeal.

What Is Fund Flow OS?

Fund Flow OS is an AI-native agentic operating system for real estate capital raising and fund management. Built on three core modules, Flow AI for autonomous investor outreach, Flow Guard for 506(b) and 506(c) compliance tracking, and a Private Lender Database of 9 million county-verified records, it covers the full investor lifecycle: finding new capital, nurturing relationships compliantly, and managing active investors from your first private lender to $100M AUM.

I built Fund Flow OS because I lived the problem. After closing 70 deals in six months and building PF Capital, I had a growing network of private lenders and zero real system to manage them. Every tool I tried solved part of the problem. None of them solved finding new capital and staying compliant while doing it.

Fund Flow OS is built around three things that most investor management platforms skip entirely:

Flow AI acts as your autonomous employee. It drafts outreach in your voice, sends it, tracks responses, and tells you exactly who to follow up with today. Most platforms give you a CRM and leave you to do the work. Flow AI does the work.

Flow Guard tracks every investor touchpoint as legal compliance evidence. For 506(b) raises, you need to prove a pre-existing substantive relationship before presenting deal-specific information. Flow Guard timestamps every interaction, tracks relationship status (Red, Yellow, Green), and keeps your legal defense ready if the SEC ever asks. Our co-founder Seth, an SEC attorney, built the compliance logic directly into the product architecture.

The Private Lender Database gives you access to 9 million county-verified private lender records across the US. If you've tapped your existing network and need to find new capital sources, this is the only platform with that built in.

The platform scales from your first private lender all the way to a fully structured fund with distributions, waterfalls, and investor reporting. Operators managing $20M AUM use it. Operators on track toward $100M AUM use it. It's not a starter tool you graduate out of.

Fund Flow OS combines investor management, AI-powered outreach, and compliance tracking in one operating system.

Here's what that looks like in practice:

Flow AI vs. manual CRM. In Cash Flow Portal, you log into the CRM, find an investor, write an email, send it, manually log the activity, and hope you remember to follow up. Flow Guard vs. no compliance layer. If you're raising under 506(b), every investor touchpoint is potential legal evidence. 9M lender records vs. no sourcing tool. Cash Flow Portal helps you manage the capital you already have access to. Fund Flow OS's Private Lender Database gives you a path to capital you haven't tapped yet. For operators working to grow from $5M to $50M, that difference is the whole game.
