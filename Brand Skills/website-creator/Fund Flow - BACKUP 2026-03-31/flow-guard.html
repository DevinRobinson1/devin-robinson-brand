Relationship status

The traffic light system.

Flow Guard automatically classifies every contact's relationship status and controls what outreach is allowed.

Red -- No Relationship

Outreach blocked

New contacts from data enrichment or cold lists. No substantive pre-existing relationship established. Fund-related outreach is automatically blocked.

Yellow -- Building

General outreach only

Active conversations happening. Relationship building in progress. Flow Guard allows general engagement but screens for solicitation language.

Green -- Substantive

Full outreach enabled

Substantive pre-existing relationship verified through documented interactions. Full fund-related outreach allowed with complete audit trail.
