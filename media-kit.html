About Me

The Short Version

Copy-paste ready bios for your show notes, event program, or website.

From the streets of Los Angeles to the thriving communities of Charlotte, NC, Devin Robinson's journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. As a serial entrepreneur with a diverse portfolio of 16 ventures since 2013, his career spans a global photography company with projects in 27 countries to spearheading a dynamic real estate investing company and fund in the Southeast United States.

At the heart of his real estate endeavors lies a commitment to "Experiential Investing" -- creating unique, meaningful investment experiences backed by tangible real estate assets, offering high returns to investors.

"Your lived experience is your competitive advantage."

Now settled in Charlotte with his wife and three adopted children, Devin's life is dedicated to more than just business success. His family's journey through fostering and adoption has ignited a strong desire to address the affordable housing and foster care crises in the United States.

His Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG) is transformative: to impact the lives of 10,000 families by providing 1,000 units of affordable housing across 10 states and wield enough influence to drive significant change in the foster care system and affordable housing programs.

Devin's audience is diverse, ranging from individuals interested in passive real estate investing to those eager to learn about the intricacies of the industry. He strikes a balance between professionalism and personal connection, believing that the most profound impacts are made through authentic, meaningful interactions.
